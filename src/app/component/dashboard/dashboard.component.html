Welcome to RyDe.

A ride sharing platform




Usage

  1. Need a ride: Click I need a RyDe then enter the details.
  2. Have a ride: Click I have a RyDe then enter the details.
  3. Approve rides: GO to my ride select the ride and accept enough passengers.
  4. Get Confirmation: Once the ride has been approved, you get a confirmation email.
